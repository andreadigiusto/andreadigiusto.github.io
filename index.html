About me

I am a Ph.D. student in the Mathematical Communication Theory group at the Eindhoven University of Technology, under the supervision of Alberto Ravagnani. My research interests lie in the algebraic theory of block codes, in particular the study of parameters and higher invariants (such as generalised weights). I am also interested in the structure of codes correcting arbitrary error sets, different from e.g. the classical Hamming ball in the Hamming metric.
